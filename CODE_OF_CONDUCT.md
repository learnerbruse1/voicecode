# Code of Conduct

VoiceCode should be a welcoming, practical, and safety-minded project.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept feedback when your words or actions cause harm.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Prefer clear technical evidence over personal criticism.
- Respect privacy when sharing logs, audio, screenshots, or system details.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Abusive language directed at contributors or users.
- Security reports that include unnecessary public exploit detail before a fix is available.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately through the security/contact channel listed in `SECURITY.md`. Maintainers will review reports in good faith and may remove content, limit participation, or take other actions needed to protect the community.
